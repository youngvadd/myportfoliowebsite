Excel Project

This project looks at product performance during 2019 and 2021 period from GameZone store dataset.

Here are the questions I was interested in answering:

1) How did total revenue dollars across all products perform during 2019-2021?

2) What are the best and worst performing products?

3) Which year had the highest sales?

4) What are the best and worst performing marketing channel?

I took the following steps to create my analysis:

View the excel document

1) Pulling data from Gumroad.

2) Identifying any duplicates, missing values or inconsistent date format.

3) Creating an issue log table recording all issues in the data, magnitude and resolutions.

4) Creating a duplicate store data table for a cleaned data table.

5) creating pivot tables to aggrigate KPIs.

6) Making a deep dive analysis in Tableau.

Analysis from excel insights:

1) Total sales across 2019-2020 is ~ $6.1M Ranging in monthly sales from $80K to $549K (Finance team).

2) Best Performing product is Gaming Monitors at $1.9M in total sales and the worst performing is Gaming Headset at $800 (Product team).

3) Worst performing product category is Headset which is responsible for 0.01% of sales revenue and Gaming monitors making 32% of sales revenue (Product team).

4) December 2020 saw a large spike in sales. Seems like Fall and winter months before the new year perform best. Maybe related to holiday season or promotions at the time (Marketing team).

5) All products had a significant spike in sales in 2020 onwords, following the general trend in total sales. This indicates a company-wide impact ( most likely due to COVID-19) (Finance team).

6) Almost all products exhibited a spike in December 2020 - look into promotions or campaigns that happened at this time (Marketing team).

7) 2020 the company had 164% increase in sales revenue compared to 2019 sales revenue (Finance team).

Deep dive analysis from Tableau:

Sales more than double in early in 2020, with all time high in September and December 2020. Sales significantly dropped in February 2021. I recommend to do sanity checks on key dimensions to investigate if there's a clear driver.

Gaming monitors, Playstation 5s and Nintendo Switch are the main drivers of the dip. They all exhibit the same plateauing behaviour in 2020 and 2021. I recommend to Look into these products by region and marketing channel.

Direct is the main driver of sales. All other channels pale in comparison. I recommend to Investigate with the marketing team on email and social media strategies.

All regions exhibits similar dips in 2021 indicating a global/macro trend. I recommend to Refer to the product split as the main dimension.

While all three top products exhibits dips in early 2021, Playstation 5s had a major dip in direct traffic in the beginning of 2021 compared to other products and marketing channels. I recommend to Investigate drop in direct Playstation 5s sales in North America.

For Playstation 5s, the drop is mostly contained to North America region and direct traffic. This may indicate a shift in trends or copmetitors there.
