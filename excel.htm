Excel Project

This project looks at product performance during 2019 and 2021 period from GameZone store dataset.

Here are the questions I was interested in answering:

1) How did total revenue dollars across all products perform during 2019-2021?

2) What are the best and worst performing products?

3) Which year had the highest sales?

4) What are the best and worst performing markeying channel?
